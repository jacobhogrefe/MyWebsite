Moon Valley

Moon Valley is a modification to an existing Java game developed for me and my partners' Intro to Software Development class. It follows the story of helping a Walrus find out who he is after a freak accident caused him to lose his memory. In your journey, you will come across 4 unique areas with plenty of quests to keep you busy.
