About Me

My name is Jacob Hogrefe, and I am currently a junior Computer Science major at Quinnipiac University. My main interests are in technology and how it affects people, anything related to Nintendo, and computers!

My interest in computers came from a young age where I took my toys apart to see how they worked. As you could maybe assume, many did not go back together properly. However, it ignited something in me to learn how things I don't understand, work. As I've grown up, I've become more intrigued on how the technology we use everyday functions both at a hardware and software level. And my love for math drove me to have a knack for problem solving, and makes coding enjoyable for me. As such I am always driven to write the best code possible, while also being conscious of making it understandable.
